Rules:

Each player roles the 3 dice until at least 2 of the dice are equal. The left over dice is the player's score. If all 3 dice are the same the value of the dice face is the players score. The player with the highest score wins the round.

Roll a 4,5,6 and you win that round automatically!

Roll a 1,2,3 and you lose that round automatically!

In the event of a tie (known as a push) the players will double their bet and re-roll the dice until there is a clear winner.

Have Fun!
